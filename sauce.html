✧₊⁺ Beverages ✧₊⁺

This is my personal favorite from Zus Coffee, it's called Caramel Cream Frappe. It’s perfect for someone with a sweet tooth like me! xP Zus Coffee is a Malaysian brand that I absolutely love to support, especially since they’re also actively supporting Palestine.

Anyway, let me share some tricks you might not know:

  1. Firstly, make sure you have the ZUS Coffee app installed on your smartphone.
  2. Zus Coffee often offers a discount deal on drinks and yes, the voucher is only available through their app.
  3. First time user will get a Buy 1 Free 1 drink.
  4. Rather than walking in, order your drinks through the ZUS Coffee app to earn loyalty points.
  5. Pay using your RHB Student Card to get a 50% off.

✧₊⁺ Games ✧₊⁺

Roblox is an online platform where people can create and play games. It’s like a virtual playground where you can explore worlds and play different kinds of games. It is a fun online game that you can play together with your friends.

Here are some suggestions of games available in Roblox that you might want to try.

‎ ‎ Break In
‎ - It is a storyline game where you have to defeat the villain. The game has different endings, either the original one or the easter ending.
Playing with your friends makes it even more fun!

‎

‎ ‎ Dress To Impress
‎ - This game is specially made for those who love dress-up games. You will be given a theme for each round and have to get ready within 5 minutes. You can customize your own outfit based on the theme such as Sci-fi or Cherry Blossoms. After finishing, other contestants will rate your outfit and the stars you earn will contribute to your rank. This is another game to play with your friends as there is a duo option available.

‎

‎ ‎ Panik
‎ - Panik is a nerve-wracking game that will make your adrenaline spike. To complete the game, you must find 3 items based on the scanner's instructions. Once you've found them, you need to locate a vent to escape. But be careful! A scary machine is chasing after you!
